## 1. General Information
This application serves as a third-party client for the **GNOME Extensions** website (`extensions.gnome.org`). This policy explains how data is processed when you use this app.

## 2. Data Collection and Storage
**The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers.** We do not use any tracking tools or analytics. All data processing occurs directly between your device and the third-party services.

## 3. Third-Party Services
* **GNOME Extensions (Required):** The app connects to `extensions.gnome.org` to browse extensions. This is a technical requirement for the app to function.
* **Gravatar (Optional/Opt-In):** This feature is **disabled by default**. If you enable the "Use Gravatar Icons" slider in the settings, your IP-Address and hashed email identifiers are sent to Gravatar.com (Automattic Inc.) to fetch profile pictures.

## 4. External Privacy Policies
Since the app interacts directly with these services, please refer to their respective policies for details on their data processing (e.g., IP address logging):
* [GNOME Privacy Policy](https://www.gnome.org/privacy-policy)
* [Automattic (Gravatar) Privacy Policy](https://automattic.com/privacy/)

## 5. Your Rights
Since no data is stored by the app developer, please contact the third-party providers mentioned above for data access or deletion requests.
